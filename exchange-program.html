Program overview

ESSEC currently has 97 exchange agreements in 34 different countries with the best business schools around the world where you are sure to ﬁnd what you are looking for!

You have the possibility:

  • To spend a period in the exchange partner university program and gain 2 to 6 months of international experience
  • To choose elective courses from the catalog of the MBA/Master programs
  • To get an opportunity to strengthen your ﬁeld of specialization.

As soon as you have cleared certain courses, you can apply to go on an exchange for 1 or 2 semesters depending on the school and available places and therefore enrich your international experience even further.

ESSEC has been awarded the Eramus Charter for Higher Education for 2014-2020.
